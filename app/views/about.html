About FictionSet.in

Fictionset.in tackles just one of the ways you might find new books to read, by organising find novels by where they are set. This means that you could read a pile of books that are set in Paris if you wanted to.

Why? For fun, interest, or if perhaps if you're planning a visit, it might help you understand something of the 'soul' of the place, like a guidebook and map will help you with the layout of it.

Between the lines

As anyone who has satified curiosity by taking a peek at a guidebook's notes on their hometown knows, places are more than just a list of sights to see and historical points of interest. The essence of place is found in the people who inhabit it, and the stories that happen there - a fountain is just a fountain until you know it's where your grandparents first met. In novels, we meet the stories that bring a place to life.

Since forever we have learned more from stories than we have in classrooms. Stories have a way of dragging us in, making subjects and places come alive. In the same way that we learn a little about ideas on morality and justice when we read Les Miserables, we also absorb something of the places and locations where books are set.
